# Persona: Advay

**Source:** Real child of the product owner
**Age:** 2 years 9 months (as of Feb 2026)
**Primary test persona for:** All games, especially multilingual and alphabet/number activities

---

## Cognitive Profile

| Capability | Level | Notes |
|:-----------|:------|:------|
| **Alphabet recognition** | ✅ Strong | Reads all English alphabets confidently |
| **Spelling** | ✅ Good | Spells simple words; can do complex words like "aeroplane" if taught repeatedly |
| **Counting** | ✅ Advanced | Counts to 150, reverse order, thousands (1000, 2000…) |
| **Reading** | ⚠️ Emerging | Recognizes letters/words, not full sentence reading |
| **Attention span** | ~5-8 minutes | Typical for age; needs variety and engagement |

---

## Language Profile

| Language | Speaking | Understanding | Reading |
|:---------|:---------|:-------------|:--------|
| **Hindi** | ✅ Conversational | ✅ Full | ⚠️ Letters |
| **Kannada** | 🔄 Learning | ✅ Good | ⚠️ Letters |
| **English** | ⚠️ Basic sentences | ✅ Good | ✅ Letters + simple words |

---

## Motor Skills

| Skill | Level |
|:------|:------|
| **Fine motor (finger tracing)** | Developing — can trace large letters |
| **Pinch gesture** | Emerging — needs practice |
| **Pointing/tapping** | ✅ Reliable |
| **Sustained hand tracking** | ⚠️ Drops hand frequently |

---

## UX Expectations

- **Voice guidance essential** — can't read instructions, needs to hear them
- **Large targets** — 15%+ of screen width minimum
- **No timers** — creates anxiety at this age
- **Multilingual support critical** — Hindi/Kannada instructions preferred over English
- **Visual cause-and-effect** — needs immediate feedback (<100ms) to understand actions
- **Encouragement on errors** — "Try again!" not "Wrong!"
- **Repetition-friendly** — enjoys repeating successful activities

---

## Testing Scenarios

1. **"Find the letter"** — Can Advay locate and trace अ / ಅ / A?
2. **"Switch language"** — Can a parent switch to Hindi/Kannada without confusion?
3. **"Start without help"** — Can Advay start a game using only voice + visual cues?
4. **"Recovery"** — What happens when Advay drops his hand or looks away?
5. **"Celebrate"** — Does success feedback make Advay smile and want to continue?
